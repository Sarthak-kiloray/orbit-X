Assembled by experts at the Union of Concerned Scientists (UCS), the Satellite Database is a listing of the more than 2000 operational satellites currently in orbit around Earth.

Orbit-X

Our team Baby Yoda aims to create a project under the challenge “Orbital Sky” from the Connect Track.

We plan to develop user interactive tools for a basic understanding of satellites in the space around us, and their purpose. Using data visualisation and web development techniques, we will create an interactive and informative website.

And using Virtual Reality technology, we plan to develop an explorative and interactive application for users to get a proper view of the satellites in the orbit and further information
